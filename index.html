THE ESTIMATED COMPLETION TIME OF THE PROJECT IS 4 WEEKS

Phase 1: Design (2 weeks)
Starting with a kick-off meeting, then homepage design, any iterations, designs of remaining pages and sign-off on final designs. You'll be handed out templates of websites to choose from, so we can pre-define the layout of things. Detailed information regarding design options will be provided. From layout, to colors, to typography, to interactivity. Everything we decide on and do should fit the canvas correctly.

Phase 2: Development (1 week)
Development of the designed pages we agreed on.

Phase 3: Responsive Design & Launch (1 week)
Development of responsive versions; website launch; training session.

Extras:
I can provide any additional services outside of the scope for a small additional price. These can be any additional designs, changes to the website, additional training, brainstorming sessions, etc.

Payment structure:
20% upfront
40% after the client approves the website designs
40% after the website is built and published
Payment options: Bank Transfer, Paypal, World Remit, Ko-fi (lol)

Some additional info
Even though there are free options out there, there's a chance you might wish to have assets that require expenses. Assets such as paid fonts, stock photos, third-party tools, etc. It would help result in a more personalized website, due to less accessible assets. If there's a wish for such, it will be discussed before making any purchases. This portfolio page uses none of those, still looks pretty though, right?

What if?
If the freelancer cancels the project after it has started, the client will pay only for the produced work it intends to use. If the client cancels the project after it has started, the client will pay for the time the freelancer has worked on the project. Even if the client does not intend to use the produced work.
